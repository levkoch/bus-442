The Bus Curse:
Investigating King County Bus Lateness

By Lev Kochergin & Sonya Outhred · March 2026

Every commuter student at UW can relate to the bus curse: it seems that regardless of when you arrive to the bus stop, the universe will force you to wait ten minutes before you get picked up to your destination. Sound Transit bus routes intend to provide an effective system to funnel the 4.15 million Seattle metro area residents to their destinations. We explored showing which bus routes are active throughout the week.
